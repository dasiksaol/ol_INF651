About

Typingtest is a small typing practice tool inspired by modern typing websites. It is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and demonstrates JavaScript concepts such as events, DOM manipulation, timing, state management, and localStorage.

How it works

  • Select a time limit, mode (words or sentences), and theme.
  • Press Start and begin typing when the text appears.
  • The app tracks your raw WPM, adjusted WPM based on errors, and accuracy in real time.
  • At the end of the test, results are shown and your best stats are stored locally in your browser which can be accessed in "stats" page

FAQ

WPM stands for words per minute. Raw WPM is calculated from all characters you type. Adjusted WPM subtracts the impact of errors.

Accuracy is the percentage of correctly typed characters out of all characters typed. For example, if you type 100 characters and 90 of them are correct, your accuracy is 90%.

Your best WPM, best accuracy, and the total number of tests completed are stored using localStorage in your browser. You can find them in "Stats" page

The site uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It demonstrates event listeners, DOM updates, timing with setInterval, arrays and objects for data, conditional logic, and browser storage.
